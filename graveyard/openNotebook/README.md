# Open Notebook

- Source: https://github.com/lfnovo/open-notebook
- License: [MIT](https://github.com/lfnovo/open-notebook/blob/main/LICENSE)
- Alternatives: Google Notebook LM, Obsidian, Notion, Logseq, RemNote, Roam Research

## Features

- **Privacy-First Research**: Complete control of your research data with local storage and no cloud dependencies
- **Multi-Modal Content Support**: Import and process PDFs, videos, audio files, web pages, Office documents, and more
- **AI Provider Flexibility**: Support for 16+ AI providers including OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, Google, LM Studio, Groq, and more
- **Professional Podcast Generation**: Advanced multi-speaker podcast creation with customizable Episode Profiles (1-4 speakers)
- **Intelligent Search**: Full-text and vector search capabilities across all your research content
- **Context-Aware AI Chat**: Have conversations with AI powered by your specific research materials
- **Multi-Notebook Organization**: Manage multiple research projects seamlessly with separate notebooks
- **Content Transformations**: Powerful customizable actions to summarize, extract insights, and process content
- **Comprehensive REST API**: Full programmatic access for custom integrations and automation
- **Citations & Source Tracking**: Get AI answers with proper source citations and references
- **Three-Column Interface**: Organized workspace with Sources, Notes, and Chat sections
- **Password Protection**: Optional authentication for secure public deployments
